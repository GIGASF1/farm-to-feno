Educational Tool Disclaimer

This application is for educational, referral optimization, and population-health planning purposes only.

All data presented represents simulated seed data for demonstration purposes only. This application is NOT intended to:
- Diagnose, treat, or cure any medical condition
- Replace clinician judgment or clinical decision support tools
- Constitute a medical device or FDA-cleared diagnostic aid
- Provide validated epidemiological data for operational use

All clinical decisions must be made by qualified, licensed healthcare providers using validated clinical data from authoritative sources.

Real-world use of opportunity scores, referral recommendations, or care coordination guidance requires validation against:
- Current-year CDC BRFSS county-level asthma data
- Ohio Department of Health OHFLAC surveillance data
- CMS Medicare Geographic Variation data
- Ohio Medicaid encounter data
- HRSA facility data
- Validated drive-time routing data

Data Use: Upload only de-identified patient data. Do not upload any Protected Health Information (PHI) as defined by HIPAA.

For questions: contact the project team.
